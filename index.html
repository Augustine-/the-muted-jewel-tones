Raena, Sandy, and Rose first met at the School Of The Arts, in San Francisco California. They've all studied singing and music, and finally got together to make sweet song in 2014, playing at local venues such as Sweet Inspiration, The Lost Church, Hotel Utah, and The Milk Bar.

Their music is a sweet 'n spicy blend of comedy, drama, and three-part harmony, each of the band members brings their own unique flavor to the mix.
